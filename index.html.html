What We Do

Envirosphere bridges the gap between environmental science and clinical medicine through research, policy development, and knowledge translation

🩺

Clinical Consultation Services

Expert advisory services for complex environmental health cases, clinical decision support, and case review for healthcare providers and medical institutions addressing environmental exposures and related health outcomes.

📋

Clinical Guidelines & Policy

Development of evidence-based clinical guidelines, policy recommendations, and best practice frameworks for environmental health assessment, diagnosis, and treatment protocols in healthcare settings.

🔬

Research & Analysis

Original research in environmental medicine, systematic reviews, meta-analyses, and translational research projects. Collaborative partnerships with academic institutions, government agencies, and healthcare organizations.

🎓

Education & Training

Continuing medical education programs, professional development workshops, academic seminars, and public education initiatives on environmental health topics for diverse audiences from clinicians to policymakers.

Research Focus Areas

Current research initiatives and areas of clinical investigation

Toxic Exposures

Heavy metals, pesticides, industrial chemicals, and endocrine-disrupting compounds

Indoor Environmental Quality

Mold, air pollution, volatile organic compounds, and building-related illness

Climate Change & Health

Heat-related illness, vector-borne diseases, and climate adaptation strategies

Occupational Medicine

Workplace exposures, occupational disease prevention, and industrial hygiene

Chemical Sensitivity

Multiple chemical sensitivity (MCS), idiopathic environmental intolerance

Sustainable Healthcare

Green healthcare practices, reducing medical footprint, eco-friendly interventions

About Envirosphere

🌍

Envirosphere is an independent think tank dedicated to advancing the field of environmental medicine through interdisciplinary research, evidence-based policy development, and clinical innovation.

Founded by leading experts in environmental health, toxicology, and clinical medicine, we serve as a bridge between scientific research and practical application, informing healthcare practice, public policy, and medical education.

Our Expertise

• Board-certified physicians and PhD researchers
• Multidisciplinary team: medicine, toxicology, epidemiology, public health
• Published extensively in peer-reviewed medical journals
• Advisors to healthcare institutions and government agencies
• Partnerships with leading academic medical centers
